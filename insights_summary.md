## 6. Recommendations (Top Priorities)
1. **Operational:** Target bed occupancy to 75% through demand smoothing and referral management.  
2. **Workforce:** Implement shift-optimization algorithms and cross-training programs.  
3. **Clinical:** Establish surgeon scorecards and post-op care bundles for high-complication procedures.  
4. **Financial:** Review billing and payer negotiation strategies; prioritize high-margin services.
